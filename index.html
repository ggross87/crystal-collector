How to play!

  • Click any of the crystal buttons below to generate a random total score that match the random number generated at the start of the game.

  • By clicking the button it gives you a random number between 1 and 12 to add to your total score.

  • If your total is within 10 points of the random number to match then you win. You also win if you match.

  • Be careful not to total your score above the number to match or you loose!
